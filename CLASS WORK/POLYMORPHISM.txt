Polymorphism.

Poly -> Many
morphism -> Forms

It is comnination of many forms.

There are 2 types of Polymorphism.

1) Compile Time Polymorphism.
	
	- it is also known as Static Polymorphism or Early Binding.

e.g Method Overloading and Operator Overloading.


2) Run Time Polymorphism.

	-it is also known as Dynamic Polymorphism or Late Binding.

e.g Method Overriding.
